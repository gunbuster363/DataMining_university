         <div class="entry">
          <p id="review_52630137">
           Normally stay at the Shag-ri-la but as we got a better deal (via amex) to stay at the MO we booked harbour view rooms and couldnt have been happier. The view is fantastic and the building site not really a problem (not lit up at night - and excellent insulation means you cant hear it during the day).
           <br />
           <br />
           Spa/Gym - Used the spa and would reccomend although try to book before you arrive as it was hard to find a convenient appointment. The pool although lovely - is TINY. The gym is well equipped - and they were kind enough to lend my husband an entire kit (shoes socks t shirt &amp; shorts!).
           <br />
           <br />
           We used the hotel transfer on arrival which worked perfectly. When we returned to the MO two weeks later - we took the train - which i would highly reccomend.if you dont have lots of luggage. It was clean, quick, efficient (runs every 15 mins) and cheap. It arrives into the IFC mall and is a five minute walk to the MO - you dont even have to go outside.
           <br />
           <br />
           We booked too late to get a table reservation on Christmas day at the MO Grill - however we went to Cafe Causette and loved it. Honestly one of the most fantastic dinners Ive ever enjoyed - and with carol singers, crackers and children running around - it was perfect. Highly, highly reccomended.
          </p>

          <p id="review_52450758">
           Could not say enough good things about the Mandarin. With my trip to HK being mixed, both business and pleasure, the hotel was ideally located. I have usually stayed at the Conrad, but decided I needed a change. The lobby is breathtaking and the service very attentive from the moment you arrive. I had a very large room which was impressive, especially the wet room with an amazing shower and tub. The bed was comfy and the choice of entertainment channels very good for Asia.
           <br />
           <br />
           I had drinks one night in the Captains Bar in the lobby which served great cocktails, but beware it has a dress code. This didn't bother me at all, and it is nice to be around a crowd of nicely dressed travellers and locals. The bar on the top floor of the hotel is ideal for a romantic evening with that special someone, or a great gin and tonic and some conversation with the bartenders (as was my case). I also had a chance to eat in the steakhouse one night and highly recommend it. IT was a mix of travellers and local business folks, but the food was worth the trip, if not a bit stuffy. Service, again, was excellent.
           <br />
           <br />
           Gym was excellent as was the spa. I was amazed at the amount of cardio equipment and the fact that it was all new.
           <br />
           <br />
           I can see this hotel being ideal for business or leisure, and also for a solo traveller or family. While there I saw quite a few families with children.
           <br />
           <br />
           One additional note. The hotel is situated so that I rarely ventured anywhere I didn't walk. I highly recommend walking as a way to see HK. Enjoy!
          </p>
